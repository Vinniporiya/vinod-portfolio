Research journey

University of Arizona · Dept. of Physiology · Lab of Prof. Mingyu Liang · Tucson, AZ
  • Multi-species atlas: Worked on snRNA-seq + ATAC-seq analysis across different species; built first multi-species single-cell hypertension reference (manuscript under review, Science).

  • ALS transcriptomics: Characterised aberrant expression and splicing from the NY Genome Center ALS Consortium using OUTRIDER, FRASER, DESeq2; built ALS classification framework.
